# GDPR Compliance

## Applicability
This policy applies to users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom.

## Data Controller
BaseUSDP acts as data controller for off-chain personal data processed through the protocol.

## Legal Basis for Processing
- **Contract performance:** Processing necessary to provide the service
- **Legitimate interest:** Security monitoring and fraud prevention
- **Consent:** Optional analytics and marketing communications

## Your Rights
Under GDPR, you have the right to:
1. **Access** — Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Rectification** — Correct inaccurate personal data
3. **Erasure** — Request deletion of your personal data
4. **Restriction** — Limit how we process your data
5. **Portability** — Receive your data in a structured format
6. **Objection** — Object to processing based on legitimate interest

## How to Exercise Your Rights
Send requests to privacy@baseusdp.com. We respond within 30 days.

## Data Protection Officer
Contact our DPO at dpo@baseusdp.com for privacy-related concerns.

## International Transfers
If data is transferred outside the EEA, we ensure adequate safeguards through Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms.
