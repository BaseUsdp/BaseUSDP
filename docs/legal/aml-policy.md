# Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Policy

## Purpose
This policy outlines BaseUSDP's approach to preventing money laundering and terrorist financing in compliance with applicable regulations.

## Risk Assessment
BaseUSDP conducts ongoing risk assessments of its privacy-preserving payment protocol. While the protocol provides transaction privacy, it includes safeguards against illicit use.

## Safeguards
1. **Transaction Limits:** Individual deposits and transfers are capped at defined tiers to reduce risk
2. **Rate Limiting:** Unusual transaction patterns trigger automated rate limits
3. **Compliance Hooks:** The protocol supports optional compliance disclosure for regulated entities
4. **Cooperation with Law Enforcement:** We cooperate with lawful requests from authorities

## User Responsibilities
Users must:
- Not use the protocol for illegal activities
- Comply with local AML regulations
- Report suspicious activity they encounter
- Maintain records for tax and compliance purposes

## Monitoring
BaseUSDP does not monitor individual transactions (by design, as a privacy protocol). However, aggregate metrics are monitored for unusual patterns that may indicate systematic abuse.

## Updates
This policy is reviewed and updated regularly to reflect regulatory changes and best practices.
