# KYC Requirements

## Current Status
BaseUSDP is a decentralized protocol that does not currently require Know Your Customer (KYC) verification for basic usage. Users connect with their existing blockchain wallets.

## Tier-Based Approach
As regulatory requirements evolve, BaseUSDP may implement tiered KYC:

### Tier 1 — No KYC (Current)
- Wallet connection only
- Limited to lower deposit tiers (1-10 USDC)
- Standard rate limits apply

### Tier 2 — Basic Verification (Planned)
- Email verification
- Access to higher deposit tiers (100 USDC)
- Increased rate limits

### Tier 3 — Full Verification (Planned)
- Government ID verification
- Access to maximum deposit tiers (1,000+ USDC)
- Merchant API access
- Priority support

## Merchant KYC
Merchants integrating BaseUSDP for payment acceptance will be required to complete business verification including:
- Business registration documents
- Beneficial ownership information
- Website verification
- Intended use case description

## Data Handling
Any KYC data collected will be:
- Encrypted at rest and in transit
- Stored in compliance with GDPR and applicable regulations
- Deleted upon request (subject to legal retention requirements)
- Never shared without explicit consent or legal obligation
