# Cookie Policy

## Overview
BaseUSDP uses minimal cookies to provide core functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

## Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies
| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `session_token` | Authentication session | 24 hours |
| `network_preference` | Selected blockchain network | 1 year |
| `theme` | Dark/light mode preference | 1 year |
| `locale` | Language preference | 1 year |

### Functional Cookies
| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `deposit_notes` | Encrypted deposit note backup | Persistent |
| `tutorial_completed` | Track onboarding progress | 1 year |

### Analytics Cookies (Optional)
If you consent, we may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand usage patterns:
| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `analytics_consent` | Records your analytics preference | 1 year |

## Third-Party Cookies
BaseUSDP does not embed third-party cookies. Wallet providers (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet) may set their own cookies through their extensions.

## Managing Cookies
You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that clearing essential cookies will log you out and may remove locally stored deposit notes.

## Local Storage
In addition to cookies, BaseUSDP uses browser local storage for deposit note management and application state. This data never leaves your browser.
