# What is USDC?

## Overview
USD Coin (USDC) is a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US dollar. Each USDC is backed by cash and short-term US Treasury bonds held in regulated financial institutions. USDC is issued by Circle.

## Why USDC for Privacy Payments?
BaseUSDP uses USDC as its primary currency for several reasons:
- **Stable value:** No price volatility means predictable payment amounts
- **Wide acceptance:** USDC is one of the most widely accepted stablecoins
- **Regulatory compliance:** Issued by a regulated entity with transparent reserves
- **Native on Base:** USDC has native issuance on Base, avoiding bridged token risks

## USDC on Base
- Contract address: 0x833589fCD6eDb6E08f4c7C32D4f71b54bdA02913
- Decimals: 6
- Standard: ERC-20

## Obtaining USDC on Base
1. Purchase on Coinbase and withdraw to Base
2. Swap ETH for USDC on a Base DEX (Uniswap, Aerodrome)
3. Bridge USDC from Ethereum mainnet using the official CCTP bridge
4. Receive USDC from another Base user

## USDC vs USDT
While both are dollar-pegged stablecoins, BaseUSDP chose USDC for its transparency, regulatory standing, and native Base support. USDT support may be added in future versions.
