# Treasury Management

## Overview
The BaseUSDP treasury holds protocol-generated fees and is used to fund development, security, marketing, and community initiatives.

## Revenue Sources
- Protocol fees from private transfers (0.3%)
- Merchant API subscription fees (planned)
- Grants and donations

## Treasury Allocation Guidelines
| Category | Allocation | Description |
|----------|-----------|-------------|
| Development | 40% | Core protocol development and maintenance |
| Security | 25% | Audits, bug bounties, and security infrastructure |
| Operations | 15% | Infrastructure, hosting, and operational costs |
| Community | 10% | Grants, bounties, and community programs |
| Reserve | 10% | Emergency fund and strategic reserves |

## Multisig Wallet
The treasury is managed via a multi-signature wallet requiring 3-of-5 signatures from core team members. The multisig address and all transactions are publicly viewable on BaseScan.

## Spending Process
1. Expenditures under $1,000 can be approved by any two core team members
2. Expenditures $1,000 - $10,000 require a core team vote
3. Expenditures over $10,000 require community governance approval

## Transparency
- Monthly treasury reports published in docs/governance/
- All transactions visible on-chain
- Quarterly financial summaries posted to GitHub Discussions

## Audit
Treasury management processes are audited annually by an independent third party.
