# Voting Process

## Overview
As BaseUSDP transitions to community governance, this document outlines the planned voting process for protocol decisions.

## Proposal Types

### Protocol Parameter Changes
Changes to fees, deposit limits, tier structures, and other protocol parameters.
- **Voting period:** 7 days
- **Quorum:** 10% of eligible voters
- **Threshold:** Simple majority (>50%)

### Protocol Upgrades
Smart contract upgrades, new features, and architectural changes.
- **Voting period:** 14 days
- **Quorum:** 20% of eligible voters
- **Threshold:** Supermajority (>66%)

### Treasury Allocation
Spending from the protocol treasury for development, marketing, or grants.
- **Voting period:** 7 days
- **Quorum:** 15% of eligible voters
- **Threshold:** Simple majority (>50%)

## Voting Eligibility
Eligible voters will include:
- Protocol token holders (when launched)
- Active contributors with merged PRs
- Registered merchants with transaction volume

## Voting Mechanism
- Snapshot-based off-chain voting for gas-free participation
- On-chain execution for approved proposals
- Delegation supported for voters who prefer to delegate their vote

## Emergency Proposals
For critical security issues, the core team retains emergency veto power with a 48-hour public explanation period.
