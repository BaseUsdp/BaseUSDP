# Ambassador Program

## Overview
BaseUSDP Ambassadors are community members who help spread awareness, provide support, and gather feedback about the protocol.

## Benefits
- Early access to new features and beta releases
- Direct communication channel with the core team
- Ambassador-exclusive events and calls
- Recognition in the CONTRIBUTORS.md file
- Potential token allocation when governance launches

## Responsibilities
- Create educational content (articles, videos, tutorials)
- Help answer community questions on GitHub Discussions
- Organize or participate in local meetups
- Provide user feedback and feature suggestions
- Report bugs and usability issues

## Requirements
- Active community participation for at least 1 month
- Understanding of BaseUSDP's technology and values
- Positive reputation in the crypto community
- Commitment of at least 5 hours per week

## How to Apply
Submit an application at baseusdp.com/ambassador or email community@baseusdp.com with:
1. Your background and experience
2. Why you want to be an ambassador
3. What content or activities you plan to create
4. Links to your social media or previous work

## Code of Conduct
Ambassadors must adhere to the BaseUSDP Code of Conduct. Violations may result in removal from the program.
