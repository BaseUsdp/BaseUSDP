# Community Events

## Regular Events

### Monthly Community Call
- **When:** First Wednesday of each month, 6:00 PM UTC
- **Where:** Google Meet (link shared in Discussions)
- **Format:** 30-minute update from core team, 30-minute open Q&A
- **Recording:** Published to GitHub Discussions after the call

### Weekly Dev Standup
- **When:** Every Monday, 3:00 PM UTC
- **Where:** Internal (open to maintainers and active contributors)
- **Format:** Quick updates on current development progress

## Hosting a Community Event
Want to organize a BaseUSDP meetup or workshop in your area? We support community-organized events with:
- Presentation materials and slide decks
- Promotional support on our channels
- Swag and merchandise for attendees
- Speaker support from the core team (virtual)

Contact community@baseusdp.com to get started.
