## Becoming a Maintainer
Maintainer status is granted to consistent, high-quality contributors who demonstrate:
1. Deep understanding of the codebase and architecture
2. History of thoughtful code reviews
3. Commitment to the project's values and goals
4. Ability to collaborate constructively with the community

## Maintainer Responsibilities
- Review and merge pull requests in a timely manner
- Participate in security incident response
- Attend monthly maintainer meetings
- Follow the release process and versioning guidelines
- Mentor new contributors
